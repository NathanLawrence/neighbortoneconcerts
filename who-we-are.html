Daniel Bernstein, Founder and Director

Daniel Bernstein is a clarinetist and aspiring arts administrator, dedicated to making the arts more relevant and accessible to 21st century audiences. Daniel is currently pursuing a Bachelor Music in Clarinet Performance at Lawrence University in Wisconsin, where he is currently leading A Tempo, an initiative committed to creating a deeper, more meaningful connection between musician and audience. A Tempo produces performances and programs at Lawrence University to help musicians and musical groups successfully connect with their audiences.

Nathan Lawrence, Technical Assistant

Nathan currently serves as Digital Content Director at NPR member station KBIA and Classical 90.5 FM in Columbia, Missouri. An avid music aficionado and devotee of the reconceived musical experience, he works to hone his skills in photography, filmmaking and data journalism.
